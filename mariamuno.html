🎯 How to play

Your turn: Click a card that matches the top card (same color, number, or symbol).
No match? Click the Draw pile. If you still can't play, turn ends.
Wild cards: Click a Wild or Wild+4 → choose a color.
UNO: When you have ONE card left, press "Call UNO!" before your turn ends.
Catch: If opponent forgets to call UNO, press "Catch UNO" to make them draw 2.
Win: Be the first to play all your cards!
